# Attribution

This repository was designed by synthesizing ideas from several public Buffett-related skill repositories and official Berkshire materials.

## Public inspiration repos

- `will2025btc/buffett-perspective`
  - strongest contribution: mental models, owner mindset, plain-language explanation style
- `BruceLanLan/buffett-oracle-analyzer`
  - strongest contribution: broader market coverage, examples, and evaluation scenarios
- `agi-now/buffett-skills`
  - strongest contribution: progressive disclosure, topic routing, and industry-playbook structure
- `MichaelRochonnn/buffett-investment-research`
  - strongest contribution: primary-source-first discipline, verdict bands, and SEC bootstrap helper idea

## Official Berkshire materials

- Berkshire Hathaway Owner's Manual
- Berkshire Hathaway annual letters
- Berkshire Hathaway annual reports
- Berkshire acquisition criteria

## Reuse policy in this repo

- The overall framework and prose in this repository were rewritten from scratch.
- The SEC helper script was adapted in spirit from MIT-licensed Buffett research helpers and refactored for this repository.
- Ideas from repos without an explicit license were treated as inspiration for structure and feature selection, not copied text.
